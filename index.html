About Me

I have an extensive background with 25 years of experience in software development including over 13 years in a supervision/manager role. I have always been a hands-on manager and continued to participate in code reviews and architecture decisions as well as diving into the code as needed. My background in Microsoft technologies has included everything from Classic ASP, ASP.NET through .NET MVC and MVVM patterns using Knockout.js/Angular.

My focus has been on developing custom .NET web applications for internal customers with my strengths around analysis and database design. I have been part of Agile teams for over 7 years, participating at all levels from team member (developer) to product owner to Scrum master.
